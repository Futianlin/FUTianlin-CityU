Supervised by [Prof. Stefano Stramigioli](https://www.utwente.nl/en/research/our-scientists/stramigioli/) and [Dr. Kenan Niu](https://research.utwente.nl/en/persons/kenan-niu/), my research primarily focuses on human-robot interaction, smart sensing, and EEG-based control algorithms.
